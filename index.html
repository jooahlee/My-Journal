The Attack of The Heels - 12/10/2022

I was in the middle of my skating class when I plopped down on the ice failing a jump my teacher asked me to do. I had butt pads so it wasn't really that painful but hurt a bit when I landed on my hip. My teacher asked me if I was ok and we moved on to spins. I held up my right foot, turned to my right still standing on my left foot and spun. Then, the fall happened. It was successful until I leaned on my heels and fell down on the back again! After I handled how to remain controlled, I moved to another jump/spin thingy. I had to rotate 180 degrees on one leg and then tap on my toe to jump. When the jump happened, I landed on my back again when my teacher said, "Wow, you sure are loving your heels today!"


The moral of the story is to be careful with your heels. Sometimes they aren't so nice.
